He finds his cat after five years disappeared and almost 2,000 km away

 

The pussycat, from Portland, was found by members of the Santa Fe animal shelter. Thanks to the chip they were able to identify it
A cat that disappeared five years ago in Portland, Oregon, has just been found in Santa Fe, New Mexico, both in the United States. The pet, named Sasha, had traveled 1,931 kilometers, it is not yet known how, but finally he has already been able to meet his owner.
Five years ago, Viktor Usov lost his cat and as time went by he got the idea that he would never see her again. Until a few days ago, he received a call from an animal shelter in Santa Fe, where he was told that they had their pet, which they had collected while wandering around the city.
In no way, that can't be my cat: it must be a mistake, "Usov replied to the animal shelter employee who contacted him. But, finally, it was his beloved Sasha, whom In the absence of a collar, his chip was scanned, where his identification number and the owner's contact information came.
"It's a mistake"
Despite the initial disbelief, they exchanged photos of Sasha when she was a kitten and how she was today. There was no doubt, it was the same cat.
She was very adventurous and loved the outdoors. I went outdoors when I wanted to. One night he did not return
The American Airlines airline offered to move the cat to Portland, accompanied by a member of the Santa Fe animal shelter, and finally, last Tuesday the reunion took place.
She was very adventurous and loved the outdoors. I went outdoors when I wanted to. One night he did not return, recalls excited Usov, who thought that the pussy was gone forever.
Now that the two are reunited and Usov plans to enjoy the return of his cat after five years. "I will take her home and love her.
